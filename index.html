Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Vietnam ETA?

A Vietnam ETA/eVisa is an electronic travel authorization allowing eligible travelers to enter Vietnam for approved purposes.

How long does processing take?

Processing times vary depending on application type and government approval timelines.

Can I apply online?

Yes, travelers can submit applications and supporting documents online.

Do you guarantee approval?

No. Final visa approval decisions are made solely by the relevant immigration authorities.
